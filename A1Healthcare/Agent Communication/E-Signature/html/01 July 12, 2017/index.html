This notification is to advise you of updates that have been made to the e-signature process in E123. These changes were requested by Chubb and are outlined below.

These new changes will be effective Monday, July 17, 2017.

1. 

The Member will click the check box next to each product listed, signifying their acknowledgment and approval of the product purchased.

 
 

 

2. 

The Member MUST "sign" their name in the box. The system will not accept "no mark" or partial marks.

 
 

 

3. 

The Member must "type" their name in the box next to Name. The system will not accept a blank box.
