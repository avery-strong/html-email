Through an association membership with Wellness Plan of America (WPA), members will have access to several money-saving benefits to offset everyday expenses.

 

Highlights

Health Advocacy

A team of personal Wellness Specialists will coordinate the journey to wellness for members. They will help navigate lifestyle benefits and continuously make the most of their membership such as:

 

•

 

Locating a healthcare provider for treatment such as Doctor, Dental, Vision, Hearing, Routine Checkups or preventative care.

 

•

 

Scheduling provider appointmentsver 2,000 veterinary hospitals across the country. Also includes Lost Pet recovery service.

 

•

 

Scheduling Blood Testing such as Cancer Screening & other preventative tests

 

•

 

Escalation to Prescription Savings Specialists who will find medications at the lowest possible price - on average savings of UP TO 85%!

Telemedicine

1-800MD's national telemedicine program provides fast and convenient access to quality medical care.

 

•

 

Available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year for a physician consultation

 

•

 

Easily accessible alternative for non-emergent Emergency Room visits, Urgent Care visits or the inconvenience of traveling to a schedule appointment with your Primary Care Physician.

Prescription Assistance Program

WPA Prescription Savings is a comprehensive program that utilizes a network of prescription resources to find you the lowest price available on your prescription medications.

 

•

 

There's no age or income limit to apply for this program and no limit on the number of medications.

 

•

 

WPA's prescription experts will assist in completion and submission of all paperwork directly to each pharmaceutical company and/or physician's office.

Pet Benefits

From care to medications to supplies, Pet Benefits can improve or sustain the long-term health of pets. Like telemedicine for humans, they have 24/7 access to certified veterinary professionals they can reach from the comfort of their home. These pet health experts can help answer questions and point them in the right direction for their pet's needs.

 

•

 

Pet Coach - provides peace of mind after hours when most veterinary offices are closed. Consult with a veterinary technician or pet health expert in real-time via phone, e-mail or chat.

 

•

 

PetAssure - receive 25% discount on in-house medical services at a network of over 2,000 veterinary hospitals across the country. Also includes Lost Pet recovery service.
