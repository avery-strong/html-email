INTRODUCING

On behalf of the American Business Association (ABA), we are excited to inform you of a new dental plan. Advanced Dental provides dental insurance benefits underwritten by United Concordia Companies, Inc. (UCCI), for individuals and families who want and/or need dental coverage.

Dental insurance is the single largest factor in determining whether a person sees a dentist regularly and regular dental care is the best way to prevent oral disease. The UC Dental insurance plan can help consumers obtain necessary oral care.

UC Dental is a fully insured dental plan that pays for services and procedures based on a graduating scale of benefits percentages that increase over a period of 3 years. Member retention is encouraged as the plan grows in value.
