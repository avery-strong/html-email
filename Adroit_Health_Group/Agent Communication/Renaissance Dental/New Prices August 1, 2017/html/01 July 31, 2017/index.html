Dear Marketing Agents,

Good news! We have made pricing adjustments and lowered the rates for Renaissance Dental. This should make the product more competitive with other comparable dental plans available in the market. These new rates will be effective August 1, 2017.

HOW THIS AFFECTS YOUR SALES

No Product Benefit Changes

There will be no change in the product benefits, only in the rates. This rate change does not affect any members that have been sold this product prior to August 1st. The old product will no longer be accessible in the system once the revised product has been turned on for sales.

Must Sell As Add-On to Core Products

The revised product will now need to be sold with a Core Product such as a Hospital Indemnity, Short Term Medical or Limited Medical plan. It can no longer be sold as a stand-alone product. We will set rules in the system to prevent it being sold as a stand-alone.
